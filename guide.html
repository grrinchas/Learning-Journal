Solo Pasta

“ Welcome to Eastbourne’s Solo Pasta Restaurant, serving amazing Italian food with your hosts Aleksandra and Chef Gianni since 2013. The restaurant, which is small and cosy, has been transformed both in terms of the menu and the decor. Chef Gianni creates his food with the finest, fresh local produce, years of experience and lots of love. The dishes are made to age old family recipes and are freshly prepared, so they can be adapted to personal taste or dietary requirements. Different menus can be prepared for special occasions to suit your wishes. Nothing is too much trouble. At Solo Pasta you will also find a good selection of fine Italian wines and first class customer service. We really pride ourselves on giving our customers a very warm welcome! The ambience is friendly and relaxed with the emphasis on what we can do to make your lunchtime or evening special. We very much hope you will join us for a quality dining experience soon and you’ll see why our customers rate us as one of the best restaurants in Eastbourne.

— Solo Pasta Pizzeria Ristorante

Thai house

“ The Thai House is a relaxing little gem in the heart of Eastbourne, located in the Little Chelsea area combining outstanding traditional cuisine and subtle authentic decor with a warm, welcoming ambience - bringing the charms of Thailand to Eastbourne. Our chefs Chaun and Fai use only the very finest and freshest ingredients; the cooking style reflects a rich mix of the traditional dishes of Thailand whilst blending in a variety of regional specialties. To complement the experience we also serve traditional homemade Thai deserts and freshly made luxury Ice creams. We also keep and excellent and well-balanced wine cellar and a good range of Thai beers and Thai whiskies. Whether you are visiting the restaurant or ordering dishes to eat at home you can be assured of the finest Thai food cooked in the traditional way prepared from the very best ingredients. The Team at The Thai House restaurant are committed to offering customers a wonderful dining experience with a taste and warmth of Thai hospitality.

— The Thai House Restaurant

Flamenco tapas

“ A warm welcome awaits you here at Flamenco, Bienvenida. Situated in the Heart of Eastbourne, just off the seafront between the Theatres and town centre. Flamenco Tapas restaurant, was first opened back in 2004, making us Eastbourne’s first, and most established Spanish restaurant. Offering, a choice of around 60 Tapas dishes as well as daily Specials and with over 20 Spanish wines and Cava’s to complement our tapas. With the restaurant set over two floors, we are ideally suited to quite romantic meals or larger family celebration’s, whatever the occasion we can make you feel as if you are in Spain. We pride ourselves in using specially imported products from artisan producers all over Spain as well as award winner local butchers and greengrocers.

— Flamenco Tapas Restaurant
